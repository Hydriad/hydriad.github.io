About me

As much as I'm willing to share with strangers online, anyway.

Hi, I'm Hayley and I live in Upstate New York, USA. I like to surround myself with books, nature, and friends.

What I really appreciate about moments in life is their transience. When seasons change, when I share a moment with a stranger, when I'm exploring a new place -- these are things I love. Because they will never happen again just as they are right now.

Outdoors, I enjoy hiking, camping, and backpacking. Cross-country skiing is my sport of choice in the winter, and ultimate frisbee in the warmer months. I'm interested in growing my own food, and aspire to maintain a large garden one day. Although I'm limited in that right now, I've had great success with indoor gardening.

I'm also an avid reader, mostly of Science Fiction, Fantasy, and Memoirs. In all of these, the escapism is what I'm after. My favorite book is The Wise Man's Fear by Patrick Rothfuss, which is sequel to The Name of the Wind.

I'm an artist and a software engineer with a passion for making beautiful things. Head to my projects page to see what I've made recently.
