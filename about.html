About me

As much as I'm willing to share with strangers online, anyway.

I love anime, and I love telling that to people I meet because it's a great way to find other unexpected fans. My favorite series is Hunter X Hunter. Anime is a gateway drug to to kpop, and I'm now hooked on that as well. I'm listening to kpop as I write this.

I'm an avid reader. The escapism is what I'm after, so I enjoy reading SciFi and Fantasy the most. My favorite book is The Wise Man's Fear by Patrick Rothfuss, which is sequel to The Name of the Wind.

I live near Boston, MA, USA with my boyfriend Kyle. I built his website, too. We've gone on many adventures together, and I look forward to more.

Outdoors, I really enjoy hiking, camping, and backpacking. Cross-country skiing is my sport of choice in the winter, and ultimate frisbee in the warmer months. I'm extremely interested in growing my own food and foraging for edible wild plants. Although I'm limited in that right now because I live in an urban area, I've had great success with indoor gardening!

I'm an artist and a software engineer with a passion for building beautiful things. Head to my projects page to see what I've made recently.
