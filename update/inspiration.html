Things that inspire me

A bunch of links to things that I find meaningful.

  • The Creative Independent

    An online publication that interviews creators about their work, creative process, and lives. A recent favorite of mine is Kiki Slugqueen on listening as a performance .

  • Ocean Vuong

    A brilliant poet and writer who puts thought behind every word, every sentence.

  • Zen Pencils

    Comic visualizations of quotations from inspiring people, arranged and illustrated by Gavin Aung Than. My favorite two are Live the Questions - Rilke and Taking the Piss - Banksy

  • Information Civics by Paul Frazee

    An essay on people's rights on networks, and how we are limited with today's centralized internet.

  • Changing Seasons by Sea Wolf

    My favorite song.

  • Bongcheaon-Don Ghost by Horang

    Read carefully and enable sound.

  • Harmless grafitti

    When they're not destroying something beautiful, I love to see how people personalize our urban environments.
