About Me

Hi, my name is Hayley. I'm an undergraduate senior attending RPI, majoring in Information Technology and Web Science and concentrating in UX Design. I have a passion for building the look and feel of applications to create a dynamic, intuitive user experience.

My Approach

1. Feel

Talk to users/clients about what they want to accomplish and feelings they aim to evoke in user experience.

2. Conceptualize

Create low-fidelity mockups, gather feedback, and edit. Lather, rinse, repeat until we're satisfied.

3. Focus In

Build the application with regular feedback along the way using the agile method.
